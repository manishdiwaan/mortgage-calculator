Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

urmortgage.online ("we", "our", "the site") is a free mortgage repayment calculator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you use the site.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All mortgage calculations are performed entirely in your browser. No loan details, financial data, or personal information is sent to any server.

Saved configurations (the "Save & Load" feature) are stored locally in your browser's storage and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Cookies & Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements on the site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Chart.js: Loaded from a CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) for rendering charts. Cloudflare may log basic access information.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: We use Cloudflare Turnstile on our Ask URMortgage chat feature to protect against automated bot abuse. Turnstile operates invisibly and collects no personal data beyond what is necessary for bot detection. By using the chat feature, you acknowledge Cloudflare's Privacy Policy and Turnstile Privacy Addendum.
  • Anthropic Claude: Our Ask URMortgage chat feature sends your questions to Anthropic's API for processing. Questions are not stored by URMortgage. Anthropic may process queries in accordance with their Privacy Policy. Do not include personal information in chat questions.
  • Cloudflare CDN & Security: All traffic passes through Cloudflare's network for performance, DDoS protection, and security. Cloudflare may process IP addresses and request metadata. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense & Advertising

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Data Storage

All calculator data remains in your browser. We have no database, no user accounts, and no server-side data storage. If you clear your browser data, any saved configurations will be removed.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

External Links

This site may contain links to external websites (e.g. lender websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website at urmortgage.online.
