  - question: "What is the Danish realkreditinstitut (mortgage credit institution) system?"

Borrowers can repay their loan at any time by buying back bonds at current market price, which can be advantageous when interest rates rise. This system is internationally regarded as one of the most stable and efficient mortgage funding models in the world."

Under responsible lending rules (ansvarlig långivningspraksis), interest-only loans are subject to stricter eligibility criteria, particularly for borrowers with a combined loan-to-value ratio above 60%, where banks and realkreditinstitutter must assess whether the borrower can sustain repayments if rates rise or circumstances change. The interest-only option can be attractive for cash-flow management but means the principal debt does not reduce during the interest-only period, which increases long-term interest costs and refinancing risk. Borrowers should model both scenarios carefully with their financial adviser before choosing this structure."
