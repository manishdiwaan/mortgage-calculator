Every property buyer makes mistakes. The expensive ones are the ones you can avoid by knowing what to watch for in United States.

## Not Getting Pre-Approved First

Shopping without knowing your budget wastes time and leads to disappointment. Get pre-approved before you start looking. See our [mortgage guide](/united-states/mortgage-guide/).

## Underestimating Total Costs

Closing costs, legal fees, inspections, and other costs add 3-8% on top of the purchase price. Our [buying costs guide](/united-states/blog/buying-costs-explained/) lists every cost.

## Skipping Inspections

A small inspection cost can save you from tens of thousands in hidden repairs. See our [inspection checklist](/united-states/blog/property-inspection-checklist/).

## Not Comparing Lenders

Going straight to one lender without comparing alternatives can cost thousands in unnecessary interest. Use our [mortgage calculator](/calculator/?country=us) to compare.

## Buying on Emotion

Stretching your budget for a property you love is a recipe for financial stress. Set a firm maximum and stick to it.
