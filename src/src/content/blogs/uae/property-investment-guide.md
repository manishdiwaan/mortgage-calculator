Property investment in UAE can be a powerful wealth-building strategy when done with proper research and planning.

## How Property Investment Works

You buy a property and rent it out, earning income from rental yield and potential capital growth. Leverage (borrowing to invest) amplifies both gains and losses.

## Rental Yield vs Capital Growth

Different markets offer different balances. Research vacancy rates, median rents, and growth trends in your target area before investing.

## Tax Implications

Property investors in UAE need to understand the tax treatment of rental income, deductible expenses, and capital gains. Consult a tax professional for your specific situation.

## Financing an Investment Property

Investment loans typically require larger down payments and may have slightly higher interest rates. Compare options with our [mortgage calculator](/calculator/?country=ae).

Over-leveraging, buying on emotion, and underestimating ongoing costs are the most common investment mistakes. Read our [mortgage guide](/uae/mortgage-guide/) for financing strategies, or check our [FAQs](/uae/faqs/).
